#Visual-Computing 

- List of vertices  
	- **v** x y z  
- List of vertex normals  
	- **vn** x y z  
- List of vertex texture coordinates  
	- **vt** s t  
- List of faces  
	- **f** v1/vt1/vn1 v2/vt2/vn2 v3/vt3/vn3...  
- List of groups  
	- **g** f1 f2 f3
